# The EYFS seven areas of learning & development

---

The EYFS framework supports children’s learning and development from birth to five, focusing on the whole child. It is divided into three Prime Areas and four Specific Areas.

---

# **Prime Areas**

These are essential foundations for all learning.

## **1. Communication and Language**

Children develop listening, attention, understanding, and speaking skills.

**Learning objectives include:**

- Listening and responding to others
- Understanding instructions and questions
- Expressing thoughts, needs, and ideas confidently

---

## **2. Physical Development**

Children learn to move confidently and understand how to care for their bodies.

**Learning objectives include:**

- Developing gross and fine motor skills
- Building strength, balance, and coordination
- Learning about healthy eating, self-care, and safety

---

## **3. Personal, Social and Emotional Development (PSED)**

Children learn how to understand themselves and relate to others.

**Learning objectives include:**

- Building self-confidence and independence
- Managing emotions and behaviour
- Forming positive relationships and showing empathy

---

# **Specific Areas**

These build on the prime areas and support deeper learning.

---

## **4. Literacy**

Children develop early reading and writing skills.

**Learning objectives include:**

- Enjoying stories, songs, and rhymes
- Recognising sounds, letters, and words
- Beginning to write for meaning

---

## **5. Mathematics**

Children develop understanding of numbers, patterns, and problem-solving.

**Learning objectives include:**

- Counting and recognising numbers
- Understanding shape, space, and measures
- Exploring patterns and simple calculations

---

## **6. Understanding the World**

Children learn about people, places, technology, and the environment.

**Learning objectives include:**

- Exploring nature and the local community
- Learning about different cultures and traditions
- Using simple technology purposefully

---

## **7. Expressive Arts and Design**

Children express ideas and creativity through art, music, movement, and role play.

**Learning objectives include:**

- Exploring materials and media
- Engaging in imaginative play
- Sharing thoughts and feelings through creative expression
