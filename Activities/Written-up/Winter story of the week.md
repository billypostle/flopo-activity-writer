# Winter story od the week
## Concept

---

**Objective:**

- To use their imagination
- Use props and puppets
- Develop their flow of speech and conversations
- Grasp the build of a story
- Develop listening and attention skills

---

**Materials needed:**

- Puppets
- Puppet stand
- Provide the story
- Resources and props that link to the story
- Cushions/ or chairs
- Basket for puppets
- Basket for reproduces

---

**Set-up**

- Set up your role-play area either chairs for washing or cushions for sitting
- Place the puppet stand where there is visibility for all
- Place your baskets behind the stand ready for the children
- Ensure you have the story to hand for reference

---

**Adult role:**

- To facilitate the opportunity for turn taking and working as a team
- To ensure all reproduces are readily available
- Develop the extension of language
- To help embed the story
- Facilitate the freedom of movement

---

**Observation cues:**

- If the children use any phrases from the story
- What language they choose including the vocabulary
- How the children work with one another or if they prefer to be solo
- The development of the children’s language
- Observe are most interested in using

---

**Safety considerations:**

- Ensure no resources or props are broken
- No loose threads on the puppets
- No resources that are too long to cause accidents or strangulation

---

**Links to EYFS (2024) learning outcomes**

Literacy - To understand the way in which story are read

Communication language- To support and extend their language

Personal social and emotional- To share and work together with peers

Listening and attention - listen to what others have to say and share within a group

---

**Alternative ethos adaptations**

Forest school- Winter fire circle - story telling around a supervised fire

Montessori-  making puppets for story telling

Reggio Emilia - story stones- painting stones for winter story telling

Steiner - winter story circle - Seasonal storytelling and verses.
