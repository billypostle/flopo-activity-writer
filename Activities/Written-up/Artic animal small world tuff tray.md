# Artic animal small world tuff tray
## Concept

---

**Objective**

- To explore animal habitats
- Learn the names of Arctic animals

---

**Materials needed**

- winter/ arctic animal figurines
- Fake snow
- Tuff tray
- Cuttings of trees
- Leaves
- Cut off wooden reels

---

**Set-up**

- clean and dry the tuff tray
- Lay your base of fake snow
- Arrange your natural resources around the trey to decorate
- Add your winter/artic figurines

---

**Adult role**

- To extend children’s knowledge on winter/artic animals
- Extend and develop vocabulary
- Support social interactions and conflicts
- Help children Explore one habitat whilst learning about others
- Help children learn about the winter season

---

**Safety considerations:**

- ensure there are no sharp material

- no resources are broken to cause cuts to children
- No resources used cause a chocking hazard

---

**Links to EYFS (2024) learning outcomes**

- understanding of the world- To explore seasons and habitats whilst learning about different animals
- Expressive art and design - exploring how to use imagination and role-play with figures
- Personal, social and emotional: sharing a space with others
