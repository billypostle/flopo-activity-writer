# New Year Goal stars
## Concept

---

**Objective**

- for children to set themselves goals they want to achieve I.e learn to ride their bike
- To build children’s positive self esteem
- To support and develop gratitude
- To develop children’s understanding on how to reach a goal (problem solve)

---

**Materials needed**

- Cut out star shapes or for older children you can have stars to draw around to create and cut own stars
- Magazines
- Pencils
- cushions / chairs

---

**Set-up**

- set up your resources first at a table ready for when you have finished your circle time
- set up a circle time with cushions or chairs a space where you can share goals as a group first
- Then engage with the resources you’ve set up allowing children to choose what to create and make their star
- Display the stars as a decoration

---

**Adult role**

- support independence
- Support children’s self esteem
- To help children wait their turn
- To support children in sharing their thoughts and feelings and understanding them

---

**Observation cues:**

- If the children understand what a goal and achievement means
- Do the children prefer to cut or write on the stars
- How the children chose to create their stars
- What challenges do the children face
- do they pride themselves in their achievements

---

**Safety considerations:**

- no pencils are sharp
- The scissors used are not too sharp and are child friendly

---

**Links to EYFS (2024) learning outcomes**

- literacy - mark making, recognising letters, creating words
- Physical development - building on fine motor skills using scissors, developing the use of mark making tools
